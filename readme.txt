
Swedish Complex Word Identification Dataset
===========================================
Contact: gretasmolenska@gmail.com
===========================================

  
DESCRIPTION
=========== 
This is a list of 4 238 Swedish lemmas with 4 annotated lexical complexity levels, as follows:

SIMPLE WORDS:
Level 1: corresponds to proficiency of a basic user
Level 2: corresponds to proficiency of a independent user

COMPLEX WORDS:
Level 3: corresponds to proficiency of a proficient user
Level 4: corresponds to proficiency of a native speaker or higher.
=======================================

FORMAT:
=======
The format of this dataset is a text file containing Swedish lemmas and their corresponding difficulty levels separated by tabs: 
<lemma>	<difficulty level>
=======================================

SOURCES:
========
Swedish CWI dataset was compiled from various sources of simple, standard and complex Swedish. 
The basis for simple words is Rivstart coursebook dicionaries for second language learners of Swedish.
The basis for complex words is standard Swedish words that do not appear in simple English datasets.
